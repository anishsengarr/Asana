Team Emerald

  • Asana was launched in 2009 by 2 Facebook Co-founders: Justin and Dustin.
  • Asana is a collaborative tasks management application that helps individuals and teams share, plan and organize their projects
  • Lets teams manage their work flows by breaking down projects into tasks to help keep everyone on the same page.
  • Free for up to 15 team members.
  • It is fast and easy to use. Shows accountability and transparency.

Key features

  1. Flagging Fast and Urgent
  2. Drag and Drop
  3. Follow-up options
  4. Search options
  5. Simplifies tasks
  6. Daily Planner

Asana fosters Efficiency

  • Asana helps you maximize productivity and stay on top of your responsibilities by keeping all your assignments in one centralized place.
  • Creating a new task is as easy as typing it in and pressing enter. You can also prioritize each task so Its easier to keep track of important projects.
  • Team Members communicate in real-time by commenting on tasks
  • Project creators will be notified (via email) when independent tasks are completed(WOW!!)

Asana is Versatile

  • The workspaces give you a place to collaborate with other teammates in real-time.
  • makes it easy to switch between project or tasks.
  • helps you stay on top of the details no matter where you are by automatically send you an update to your email. on your Asana account, and as a notification to your mobile app.
  • allows you to attach files to any task or project, which makes it easier to share finished work and additional information with group members.
